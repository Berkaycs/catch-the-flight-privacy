Catch the Flight - Privacy Policy

Catch the Flight values user privacy. This app does not collect any personal data directly. It uses Google Play Games services, which may process some basic user information (such as username and score) for achievements and leaderboards.

This data is only used to improve your gameplay experience and is not shared with third parties.

The app may use Google Play Services, which are subject to their own privacy policies.
